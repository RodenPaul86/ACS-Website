Who We Are

Not a Franchise.
Just Family.

Bob McDonough, founder and owner of Automotive Connections Services, has been working on vehicles since he was 12 years old growing up on Chicago's north side. Almost three decades of real-world experience means we've seen it all — and fixed most of it. Bob's passion for keenly understanding the engineering on practically anything that moves or carries a current has led him to the exacting expertise he shares with every client. Simply put, Bob is an automotive electrical genius.

ACS isn't a franchise. It's Bob and a team that cares about your car the way you do. Bob has passed his knowledge on to his apprentices, who work diligently to service vehicles of all makes and models all over the Chicagoland area — to factory specs, because your car deserves nothing less. No shortcuts, no guesswork, no unnecessary upsells. Just honest diagnostics and expert repairs from people who treat every vehicle like it belongs to their own family. When dealerships have exhausted their best mechanics without success, they call on ACS.

Mobility is one of ACS's strongest assets. We come to your shop, home, work, or garage — just about anywhere in the Chicago metro area — to service your vehicle.
